Nursery Section

Our Nursery Section gives young children a joyful, caring, and structured beginning where early learning, play, faith, and personal growth are nurtured every day.

Early Learning

A Gentle Start For Growing Minds

In the Nursery Section, children are introduced to learning through care, guided play, creativity, and routines that help them build confidence, curiosity, and readiness for primary education.

Play-Based Learning

Children learn through songs, games, movement, stories, and guided activities that make early education engaging and meaningful.

Care And Social Growth

We help children learn how to share, listen, communicate, and grow in confidence as part of a supportive school family.

Values From The Beginning

Nursery learners are introduced to kindness, respect, prayer, and positive habits that shape their early character development.

Nursery Experience

What Supports Our Young Learners

The Nursery Section is built around care, safety, structure, and joyful learning so that children feel secure as they take their first steps into school life.

Creative Activities

Hands-On Discovery

Children are introduced to shapes, colors, sounds, and simple concepts through fun and guided classroom experiences.

Safe Daily Routine

Comfort And Stability

Gentle routines help learners feel secure, settle well into school life, and build positive habits from an early age.

Language And Expression

Speaking And Listening Skills

Songs, stories, classroom sharing, and teacher guidance help children grow in speech, listening, and self-expression.

Nursery Events

Experiences Our Nursery Learners Enjoy

Nursery learners also take part in simple, joyful, and age-appropriate events that make school life memorable while supporting learning, confidence, and participation.

Mini Study Trips

Short educational outings introduce children to new places and help them connect classroom learning to the world around them.

Celebration Days

School celebrations help children express joy, practice participation, and build a sense of belonging in the school community.

Music And Recitation

Songs, poems, and class presentations give children early opportunities to speak, sing, and perform with confidence.

Group Learning Moments

Shared classroom events encourage interaction, listening, friendship, and positive participation in early learning.

Creativity Days

Coloring, drawing, singing, and playful classroom projects help children explore ideas and enjoy learning through creativity.

Faith And Values Activities

Age-appropriate spiritual and values-based activities help children learn prayer, kindness, obedience, and respect from the beginning.

Praise Academy is committed to nurturing children through quality education, Christian values, and a caring learning environment.
