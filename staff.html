Meet Our Team

Staff And Leadership At Praise Academy

Our staff and leadership team are committed to guiding learners with care, discipline, Christian values, and a deep passion for quality education.

Administration

School Administration

Praise Academy is guided by a leadership team committed to academic excellence, Christian formation, discipline, and the wellbeing of every learner in the school community.

Founder

Mutoni Monique

Founder of Praise Academy School, with a vision to provide quality Christian education that supports vulnerable children and nurtures them in knowledge, discipline, and the fear of God.

Spiritual Leadership

Pastor Georges Karake

A key voice in the spiritual life of the school, helping learners grow in wisdom, faith, character, and a Christ-centered understanding of education and service.

Head Master

SABITI Dan

Leading the daily life of the school with a focus on academic direction, staff coordination, student growth, and the smooth running of teaching and learning across Praise Academy.

Director Of Studies

NDUNGUTSE Bangirana Steven

Overseeing curriculum delivery, lesson quality, learner progress, and classroom excellence to ensure strong academic foundations in both the nursery and primary sections.

Director Of Discipline

Structure

Administration Structure Of The School

The school administration works in a clear leadership structure that supports decision-making, academic quality, discipline, and the daily wellbeing of learners across Praise Academy.

Top Leadership

Founder

Provides the vision, mission, and long-term direction of Praise Academy School.

School Leadership

Head Master

Coordinates the overall administration of the school and leads day-to-day operations.

Academic Wing

Director Of Studies

Oversees curriculum delivery, lesson planning, learner progress, and academic performance.

Student Conduct

Director Of Discipline

Guides student behaviour, school order, values formation, and discipline standards.

Teaching Team

Class Teachers

Teach, mentor, assess, and support learners in both nursery and primary sections.

Operations Support

Support Staff

Help maintain a safe, organized, and caring school environment for all learners.
