Donate To Praise Academy

We welcome support from friends, parents, alumni, and well-wishers who would like to help strengthen learning and care for our children. Your contribution can make a real difference in the life of the school community.

Buying Books

Support the purchase of learning materials, storybooks, classroom texts, and academic resources that help learners grow in knowledge and confidence.

Construction

Help improve and expand school infrastructure through classroom development, repairs, and the creation of safer and more effective learning spaces.

Student Living Support

Contribute toward the wellbeing of learners by supporting essential needs, welfare, and the school environment that helps children learn with dignity and care.

To support Praise Academy through any of these areas, please contact the school directly and we will guide you on the best way to contribute.
