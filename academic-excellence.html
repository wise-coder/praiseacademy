About Us

Academic Excellence At Praise Academy

Our commitment to academic excellence is rooted in strong Christian values, disciplined learning, and a caring school environment that helps every child grow in knowledge, confidence, and purpose.

Our Vision

To empower learners to acquire, demonstrate, articulate, and value knowledge that will support them as lifelong learners, guided by strong Christian values.

Our Mission

To provide affordable quality education and develop young children with active and creative minds, with a sense of understanding and compassion for others.

Our History

Founded in 2018 by Mutoni Monique in Gasabo District, Ndera Sector, Masoro Cell, Praise Academy School was established to support vulnerable children, including orphans and the needy, through quality education rooted in strong Christian values.

Excellence In Practice

How We Build Strong Learners

Academic excellence at Praise Academy is not only about results. It is about forming learners who think clearly, work diligently, grow spiritually, and develop the confidence to succeed in every stage of life.

Strong Classroom Foundations

We guide learners through structured teaching, clear academic goals, and supportive classroom practice that helps them build lasting knowledge and confidence.

Whole-Child Development

We combine academic growth with character formation, discipline, faith, and social development so learners mature in both mind and conduct.

Faith-Guided Learning

Our Christian environment encourages responsibility, compassion, integrity, and purpose as part of the daily educational journey of every learner.

Praise Academy is committed to nurturing children through quality education, Christian values, and a caring learning environment.
