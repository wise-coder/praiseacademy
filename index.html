Welcome Message From The School Administration

It is our great pleasure to welcome you to Praise Academy School. We are honored that you have taken time to learn more about our institution and what we stand for.

At Praise Academy School, we are committed to providing a safe, supportive, and inspiring learning environment where every child is given the opportunity to grow academically, socially, morally, and spiritually.

We believe education is a partnership between the school, parents, and the community. Together, we shape disciplined, confident, and God-fearing learners who are prepared for future success.

More About Us
Our Vision

To empower learners to acquire, demonstrate, articulate, and value knowledge that will support them as lifelong learners, guided by strong Christian values.

Our Mission

To provide affordable quality education and develop young children with active and creative minds, with a sense of understanding and compassion for others.

Our History

Founded in 2018 by Mutoni Monique in Gasabo District, Ndera Sector, Masoro Cell, Praise Academy School was established to support vulnerable children, including orphans and the needy, through quality education rooted in strong Christian values.
